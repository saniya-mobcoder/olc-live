# How do I run a match?

1. Open the **Match** tab in OLC Studio.
2. Select a requirement from the dropdown (for example REQ-0001).
3. Optionally choose **Rules only** or **AI-assisted rank**.
4. Click **Run match**.

OLC applies 12 hard eligibility gates first, then scores eligible talent 0–100. The shortlist shows the top recommendable talents (eligible and score ≥ 70).
