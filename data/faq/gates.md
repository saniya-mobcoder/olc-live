# Hard gates overview

All 12 gates must pass for eligibility:

1. Role + category
2. Mandatory skills
3. Full contract availability
4. Audition / showreel
5. Physical / technical
6. Mobility / work authorization / passport
7. Safety certifications
8. Medical clearance
9. Languages
10. Overnight rehearsal (if required)
11. Profile active
12. Identity + references verified

Budget, experience years, and director rating influence the soft score but are not hard gates.
