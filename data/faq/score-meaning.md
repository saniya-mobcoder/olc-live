# What does the match score mean?

The score is an explainable 0–100 weighted blend of:

- Skills 25%
- Production credits 15%
- Availability 15%
- Audition/showreel 10%
- Physical/technical 10%
- Mobility 8%
- Reliability 7%
- Safety 5%
- Budget 3%
- Language 2%

Bands when eligible: ≥85 Excellent, ≥70 Good (shortlist-ready), ≥50 Partial, else Weak. Gate failures yield Not Eligible regardless of soft scores.
